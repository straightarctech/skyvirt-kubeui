# Code of Conduct

SkyVirt KubeUI is committed to a friendly, safe, and welcoming community for
everyone, regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Our standard

This project follows the **CNCF Community Code of Conduct**, which is itself
based on the Contributor Covenant. The canonical text is maintained here:

> https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md

By participating in this project — issues, pull requests, discussions, chat,
or events — you agree to uphold that Code of Conduct.

### In short

**Examples of encouraged behavior:**

- Being respectful, patient, and welcoming.
- Assuming good intent and giving constructive feedback.
- Gracefully accepting feedback and focusing on what's best for the community.

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discriminatory jokes, or personal or political attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when
an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainers at **conduct@straightarc.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially and reviewed promptly. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce
the Code of Conduct in good faith may face repercussions determined by the other
maintainers.

For anything the maintainers cannot resolve, escalation to the CNCF Code of
Conduct Committee (**conduct@cncf.io**) is available once this project is a CNCF
project.
